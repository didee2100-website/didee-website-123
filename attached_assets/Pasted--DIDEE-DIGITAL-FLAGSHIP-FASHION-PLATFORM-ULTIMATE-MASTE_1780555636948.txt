# BRAND IDENTITY

## Brand Name

DIDEE

## Main Tagline

BUILT IN NEPAL. MADE FOR EVERYONE.

## Core Brand Statement

DIDEE brings together streetwear, casual essentials, and dresses that celebrate individuality, comfort, confidence, self-expression, and modern style.

Built in Nepal and created for everyone, DIDEE believes fashion should empower people to express themselves while feeling comfortable, stylish, and confident in everyday life.
