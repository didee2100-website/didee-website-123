Navigation & Section Scrolling Improvements

The website currently has several buttons and links that navigate to other sections, but when clicked, they scroll to the bottom or middle of the target section. This creates a poor user experience because users must manually scroll up to see the section properly.

Please update the navigation behavior so that whenever a user clicks any button or link that redirects to another section, the target section always opens from the top of that section, ensuring the content is immediately visible from the beginning.

The following links/buttons should be updated:

1. Who We Are Section
The "Shop the Collection" button currently navigates to the collection section but displays the lower portion of the section.
It should instead open the collection section from the very top.
2. Services Section
The "Shop Now" button should navigate to the collection/shop section and display it from the top.
3. Our Story Section
"Shop Collection" should open the collection section from the top.
"Who We Are" should open the Who We Are section from the top.
4. About Section
"Shop Now" should open the collection section from the top.
"Our Story" should open the Our Story section from the top.
5. Lookbook Section
The "Shop All Pieces" button should navigate to the collection section and display it from the top.
6. Home Section
The "Contact Us" button should navigate directly to the Contact section and display it from the top.
7. Other Home Section Buttons

The following buttons should all navigate correctly and display the destination section from the top:

Shop Collection
View Lookbook
Explore Now
Our Story
Collection
Discover Looks
Browse All Products

All section navigation throughout the website should use smooth scrolling and always position the user at the top of the selected section.
